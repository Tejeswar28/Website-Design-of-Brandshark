How It Works

Step 01
House owner contacts HouseWise representative and authorizes him/her to rent out the property (flat/bungalow) on the house owner's behalf
Step 02
HouseWise team inspects the property and creates a listing with attractive photos and details
Step 03
HouseWise markets the property, screens potential tenants, and manages the entire rental process
Step 04
On finalizing the tenant, HouseWise does the following; a) Police verification of the prospective tenant b) Ask for proof of employment (HR letter) from the prospective tenant
Step 05
After successful verification of the prospective tenant, HouseWise prepare the contract to be signed by both parties - house owner and the tenant. House owner can preauthorize HouseWise representative to sign on the owner's behalf
Step 06
a) Tenant transfers monthly rent to house owner's account. b) HouseWise continues to maintain property till end of the lease.
